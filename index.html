Externuating Circumstances Claims

These Claims could be a result of unwanted circumstances that a student may get faced with while under going through certain tasks such as submitting a coursework.

Students must make sure that a certain criteria is met before submitting a claim application. Failure to do so will result in a rejected application.

The application criteria is as follows:

  • ~ A student must upload evidence in a PDF or image format to provide evidence. Claims can't be processed without evidence
  • ~ Claims are disabled after a closure date, though student can still upload evidence until a final closure date

A student can submit one or more EC claims
